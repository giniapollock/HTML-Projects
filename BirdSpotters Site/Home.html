BirdSpotters

A Site For Birdwatching Enthusiasts




Welcome to BirdSpotters! We're a collected group of bird watching fanatics that scour Michigan for the best bird sightings. Please feel free to navigate our blog, where we post our findings after our monthly meetups. Weather permitted, we also offer biweekly classes so that you TOO can master the art of birdwatching!

A sample of our most popular work can be seen below. If you like what you see, head over to our Contact Us page and send us some kudos!

This particular shot happened to occur off the clock. One of our students had been on their way to their back porch to enjoy their morning coffee, and what they found was a scene of two birds possibly preening each other. We're just lucky they decided to bring their camera!

A shot by one of our very own, this little bird happened to make its way to lower ground as spring approached last year. The branches framing its small figure give this photo an incredible sense of symmetry.

The photographer commented on the title of this piece, that he felt as though these ladies were doing an elaborate dance on that roof: each in step with the other. We can see that the third dove is dipping down lower than the other. I wonder if it knows it fell out of step.
