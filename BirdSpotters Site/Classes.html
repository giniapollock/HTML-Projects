BirdSpotters

A Site For Birdwatching Enthusiasts



BirdSpotters is more than a social group. It is a learning group. Each of us has honed our skills of observation through our experiences in the field. Photography is only one piece of the equation. Consider lessons in stance and awareness to be able to take incredible shots like a pro. There are three levels, typically taught one after the other in cycles. They are broken down as the following:

  1. Birdwatching 101
    1. Creates a great beginners introduction to birdwatching
    2. Paid lunch by our senior BirdSpotters team
    3. Note: Camera required
  2. Movement Around Birds
    1. Mid-level course, however all levels welcome
    2. Teaches the crucial behaviors of bird watchers to get those up-close shots
  3. Camera Angles & Lighting
    1. An advanced course where we discuss high level photography techniques
    2. Requires completion of our "Movement Around Birds" course, as this will require previous exposure to birdwatching

Our classes are taught biweekly at the park on 5th Street, weather permitting. Each advanced course is $25/hr. Our beginner class is completely free, so please come visit us!
